About Us

Your trusted partners in educational, professional growth and development.

Our Mission

To empower professionals at every career stage to unlock their potential through personalized coaching, evidence-based educational strategies, and compassionate support. Since our founding in 2020, glideX has been dedicated to helping over 50 clients navigate complex career and education landscapes, secure promotions, and find fulfilling work that aligns with their values and aspirations.

Our approach is rooted in the belief that every career journey is unique. We blend psychological insights, industry expertise, and practical tools to help you overcome obstacles, build confidence, and thrive in a rapidly changing world of work. Whether you’re a student, a recent graduate, a working professional seeking a pivot, or someone returning after a career break, we are committed to being your partner in growth.

At glideX, we value authenticity, lifelong learning, and the courage to pursue meaningful change. Our team of certified coaches and mentors brings diverse experience across industries, united by a passion for helping you achieve sustainable success. We don’t just provide answers—we help you ask the right questions, discover your strengths, and design a career path that’s truly your own.

Recommended Books for Career Growth

Unforgettable Presence

Lorraine K. Lee

A practical guide to building executive presence, confidence, and influence at work. Learn how to communicate with impact, lead meetings, and build a powerful personal brand to stand out and advance your career.

Buy on Amazon

The Squiggly Career

Helen Tupper & Sarah Ellis

Ditch the career ladder and embrace a flexible, opportunity-driven approach. This book provides tools and exercises for navigating career changes, building transferable skills, and designing a fulfilling, non-linear career path.

Buy on Amazon

Awaken the Giant Within

Tony Robbins

Tony Robbins shares strategies to master your mind, body, and emotions to achieve your full potential. This book empowers you to take control of your career and life through self-mastery, goal setting, and perseverance.

Buy on Amazon

Managing Up

Melody Wilding

Learn practical strategies to work better with your boss and navigate workplace dynamics. This book empowers you to communicate effectively, advocate for yourself, and build strong professional relationships.

Buy on Amazon

Set Boundaries, Find Peace

Nedra Glover Tawwab

A guide to establishing healthy boundaries at work and in life. Tawwab uses research and CBT techniques to help you achieve work-life balance, reduce stress, and foster professional growth.

Buy on Amazon
